StyleSavvy – Everyday Fashion Made Simple

Site Name

StyleSavvy – The name conveys confidence and simplicity in fashion, making it easy for everyday people to look their best without stress. It’s short, memorable, and brand-friendly.

Site Purpose

The website helps users improve their everyday fashion with simple, practical advice. It will feature seasonal outfit ideas, beginner-friendly style tips, and curated trends. The goal is to inspire confidence while keeping fashion approachable and realistic for all body types and budgets.

Scenarios

  • “I don’t know what clothes to buy for my basic wardrobe. Where should I start?”
  • “What are the latest trends this season that I can wear without spending a fortune?”

Color Scheme

Rose Pink (#c54b6c): Used for headings, accents, and buttons to create a warm, stylish vibe.
Soft Cream (#fffaf6): Used for background to keep the site light and elegant.

Typography

Playfair Display (serif): Used for headings to give a classy, fashion-magazine feel.
Open Sans (sans-serif): Used for body text for readability and simplicity.
