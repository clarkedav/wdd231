Style Tips

Wardrobe Basics

Start with versatile pieces: white tee, dark denim, neutral trousers, clean sneakers, and a blazer.

Color Coordination

Pick a base (black, navy, beige) and add 1–2 accent colors. Keep patterns simple.

Fit First

The best outfit looks bad if the fit is off. Hem pants, tailor waistlines, and mind sleeve length.

Seasonal Layers

Use lightweight layers: tees → shirts → sweaters → jackets. Remove or add as needed.

Confidence

Wear what makes you feel great. Confidence is the most powerful accessory.
